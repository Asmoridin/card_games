// This deck is a bit of a one trick pony. Using Chosen of God, so the deck focuses on Drazi, so one
// can immediately get 3 influence from each won Show the Colors (or 4 even is Zhabar is in play!), or
// two influence from each Wargames. That is where the deck shines.

// Early Game: The focus is get up in influence quickly, getting Fashar to the inner circle (and Lemm out for
// Vizak's damage), and have a Sunhawk ready for a quick hit. Starting of with CoG, Vizak on the Sunhawk is
// 12 military. Hard to beat early...

// Middle Game: Get your bigger fleets out there and go to boost leadership and finding your conflicts. Pick
// on whoever has little military. Also, get out your locations, as they will be key later. The limited strikes
// are just incase you meet a REAL military deck... You don't want actual war, just to look strong, like the Drazi in the show.

// End Game: Defense in Depth will give you a quick 3 or 4 influence for the win. Play quick, and win conflicts.
// You need it, but be subtle, and wait for the border raids until you need them.

// Other Comments: This deck is weak to everything else. The key is that you will probably be pulling in the
// influence faster than it can be drained. With 4 or more players though, this is a very weak deck. We need more
// Drazi characters for this deck to ever really be a force. However, in small games, this deck shines and rarely loses.
