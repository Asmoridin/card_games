// Early Game: Turn 1 - Gain Influence Turn 2 - Ivanova. Turn 3 - Promote. It builds slowly, but this should
// let your And So It Begins definitely work. In fact, on turn 7, you should be able to build influence twice
// to get to 9 influence, use And So It Begins, replace Ivanova, and build one more time to end the turn at 10
// like the others probably. I keep The Hope of Peace to keep me strong with many characters before my cool people
// evolve. I may switch that for Rapid Growth in the future if it proves to be too slow.

// Middle Game: Get more people out for swarming opponents in the future, which will become progressively quicker
// as you build up destiny marks, thereby making characters cheaper to get out.

// End Game: Keep the snowball rolling and build a good inner circle with free promotions. Overpower with mega
// diplomacy, and gain power with Higher Calling. Go to win. Not too hard.

// Other Comments: This deck is susceptible to things like Diplomatic Blunder, that can makes Sheridan's
// diplomacy of 20+ disappear. Of course, this can hurt most decks, and I've helped relieve the problem
// with having more characters. It does have a tendency to keep higher stage characters stuck in hand at
// times, but aside from more drawing power, I don't know what I can add.
