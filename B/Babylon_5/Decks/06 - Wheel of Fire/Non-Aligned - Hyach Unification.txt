// I wanted to Horde the opponents and just overwhelm by sheer numbers. Therefore, I have tons
// of things to manipulate the deck and make characters cheap. That, plus Hyach to make Gerontocracy
// as nasty as possible to make my ambassador very nasty indeed!

// Early Game: Sponsor FoP first turn (to avoid the inevitable Airlock Accident), then next two turns
// getting Kirrin out and into the inner Circle. Then up to ten, and get Gerontocracy out ASAP, and improve
// as quickly as possible.

// Middle Game: Get out more Ambassadors, and get them into the inner circle to get most 'ghost influence'
// from FoP. The influence will enhance the speed getting out more characters, and more people to get them out.
// Win conflicts against those ill prepared, or with assistance from others to win the AoP's.

// End Game: Safety in Numbers should help give you the final power you need, but don't use it too early.
// The influence to apply helps too much to lose early.
