// The Valarius/Tolonius VII combo. It allows one to pay the standard price of a fleet enhancement,
// but have it affect all of the faction's fleets. With four different enhancements that give fleets +1
// military, along with other boosting enhancements, late in the game it can be quite scary. It is an
// evil snowball effect, especially with the Explorer cards.

// Early Game: It is a very slow opening, but by the time you get to ten influence with Tolonius in play,
// you will have a good fleet for the Valarius to enhance. You should also get a leader or two if you can.

// Middle Game: Raise tensions, and win an explorer conflict for the four cards that are free after it
// (especially the fleets) and keep making every fleet enhancement you find into a faction enhancement
// quickly and more fleets pumping out quickly.

// End Game: Surprisingly, Explore Vorlon Space is often THE winning conflict for this deck, usually often
// getting more than 60 military quickly. This, with Rise of the Republic and Centauri Prime, can allow nine
// power to hit the table at the same time, squishing many opponents.

// Other Comments: A good pick opponent, or someone who gets rid of the Valarius quickly can beat this deck
// easily. I think that's in the nature of the theme.
