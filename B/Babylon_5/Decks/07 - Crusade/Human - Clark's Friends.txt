// I have been playing lots of Drakh/Centauri and decided for a change (I only have enough good Drakh
// cards for 1 deck). Clark fit very well for that, especially when going for Secret Masters. However,
// I found that in large games, SM didn't work well at all, so Subvert the Nexus is also in there. I knew
// I needed lots of conflicts, and having lots of human and Drakh with solid intrigue or psi means lots
// of ways to gain up to the required 15 power of the 2 agendas. The weakness is against fast diplomacy
// or military, but the Drakh well rounded-ness works well to keep me faster. 

// Early Game: With this opening, you should have the coucilor out first action on turn 2, and be at
// 10 influence after turn 4, which is very quick. You can start pumping out characters and conflicts almost immediately.

// Middle Game: People hate the Drakh, so except people to come after you fast as they are ready. Therefore,
// keep going for conflicts and get up to the required 15 power. Get out a Drakh agenda fast and start
// focusing on getting control. Other speed may beat you, but you need to focus on your victory conditions.
// For Secter Masters, try to kill characters quickly, so when you get your agenda you have a shorter distance
// to go. For Subvert the Nexus, go for killing only characters with Destiny. Otherwise, save your energy for
// big steals. Gain extra influence when the opportunity shows up, because eventually someone should be able to batter you.

// End Game: Keep your influence up, but only really high if someone is threatening victory. Hurt the leader,
// and try to dodge attention (though Drakh tend to do that poorly). Stay focused on your goals of victory,
// since major victory is your only achievable goal.

// Other Comments: This deck can stand fairly well against diplomacy in a quick game, and crushed most intigue. 
// owever, military ca still be a problem, and I'm thinking about adding converter event to help that weakness
// (like Cut Supply Lines). However, it waters down the overall speed if I do it. Right now it doesn't need it.
