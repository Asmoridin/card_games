// This Mollari works well with the We'll Take Over theme. We'll Take Over is so key I needed
// two of it in the Crusade Pile. To get the two destiny marks for the Wells, one needs to make sure
// you get a destiny mark drawn (for the gambler's power). The cards with Marks are Galen, The Eye,
// Prime Minister Mollari, Emperor Mollari, and Dureena Nafeel. I removed Kiro because of his Doom Mark,
// and Dureena is in the deck, where I'm less likely to draw her for the same reason.

// Early Game: Starting with seven influence and drawing from the crusade pile is nice. You need We'll Take Over
// the second you draw it, and having a Technomage ready in the supporting row is nice to use the excess influence
// for the round. Also, play Faces in Stone shortly after drawing it, and prepare a large group for diplomatic
// and intrigue supremacy.

// Middle Game: Getting out more Mighty characters and win a conflict or two. Use dragons and other technomage
// evil on those who deserve your ire.

// End Game: Support the Mighty is the endgame card, but gain influence before you use the agenda for victory.
