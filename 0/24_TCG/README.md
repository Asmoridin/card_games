# 24 Trading Card Game

A competitive strategy game based on the hit TV series *24*. Players take on the roles of CTU agents or Insurgents, racing to complete dangerous missions and be the first to score 24 points.

## 🚀 Quick Start
*   **Deck Size:** 1 Directive card (starts in play) + 24-card deck.
*   **Starting Hand:** 5 cards.
*   **Victory Condition:** Be the first player to reach **24 points**.

## 🃏 Card Types
*   **Directives:** Define your side (CTU/Insurgents) and special abilities.
*   **Characters:** Your main tools for missions (attributes: Skill & Health).
*   **Equipment:** Attach to characters to boost stats or grant abilities.
*   **Agendas:** Powerful cards that "count down" each turn to trigger effects.
*   **Events:** One-time actions played during your turn or in response to opponents.

## ⚔️ Turn Sequence
1.  **Start Phase:** Reset characters, draw cards, and refill your **Time Pool**.
2.  **Maneuver Phase:** Move characters between the **Briefing Room** and the **Field**.
3.  **Main Phase:** Spend Time to play cards, attach equipment, or use abilities.
4.  **Mission Phase:** Declare missions to score points. Opponents can block with their own characters to initiate a **War Number** reveal!

## 🛠️ Key Mechanics
*   **Time Pool:** Your resource for playing cards. It starts at 2 and increases by 1 each turn.
*   **Skill Check:** Missions compare the attacker's Skill against the defender's. The winner inflicts wounds or scores the difference in points.
*   **Keywords:** Watch for traits like `Lethal`, `Focused`, or `Expert` which grant unique strategic advantages.
